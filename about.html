Who is Creative Cactus?

My name is Jon Desserres, and I'm the founder and lead developer at Creative Cactus Web Designs. My journey into web development began when I realized I wanted to pursue a career that allowed me to stay close to my family while doing something I was passionate about. As a former special education teacher and commercial real estate appraiser, I’ve always been driven by a desire to solve problems and help others succeed.


After teaching myself how to build websites from the ground up, I quickly realized that many small businesses were facing a common challenge: they needed a professional website but couldn’t justify the high upfront costs. Others had already invested in websites but were left with outdated designs that didn’t reflect their vision or the latest trends.


Recognizing this gap, I refined my approach to make professional web design more accessible to small businesses. Instead of requiring a large upfront payment, I offer a flexible pricing model that starts with $0 down and a manageable monthly fee. This allows small business owners to get the website they need without breaking the bank, ensuring they have a strong online presence that helps them grow.
